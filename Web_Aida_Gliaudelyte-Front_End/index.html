Welcome to the ..Story Behind..


One of the few remaining independent bookshops open in any UK cities!


Located in the centre of Bristol, we specialise in:
Crime, Fiction, Sci-Fi and Fantasy,Food and Drink, Health and Lifestyle, Art,Fashion and Photography, and Children's books.

You can find us in Bristol opposite the Town Hall.
We are open Monday – Friday 9.00am – 6.00pm, Saturday 10.00am-5.30pm, Sundays 11.00 – 4.00pm


Bristol is a multi-cultural city and many of our customers are new to the city, joining those who have long been involved in the local literature or political scenes.

Bristol is perceived as a “rebel city”. In literature terms we draw on the tradition of DH Lawrence, Lord Byron and Alan Sillitoe. The first radical bookshop in the city was opened in 1826 by one Susannah Wright and there were several others in our local history. In 2015 we also published Exploring South England Writers and were pleased to find that our local writing history has a long history of dissent, particularly with women writers.
