What is it? What i learned and my opinion
Bouldering is rock climbing stripped down to its raw essentials. Leaving behind ropes and harnesses and just using climbing shoes and a bag of chalk over safety mats, your challenge is to climb short but tricky bouldering "problems" (a route, or sequence of moves) using balance, technique, strength, and your brain. Taking part in bouldering is really exciting. Everyone can come and try it out. The easiest routes do not require previous experience, they are quite simple to accomplish. While the other sequences of moves require a person to be a bit more advanced and know specific techniques. It does not mean that without previous experience you can not try them out however, they are quite hard to accomplish. Every time I go bouldering I continue learning how to find new ways to go about "problems", always keep my eyes on a goal and try as many times as it requires to accomplish my aim-never give up! Benefits of bouldering are improved overall fitness, a lot of good emotions.
