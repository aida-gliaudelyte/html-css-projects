What is it? What i learned and my opinion
Acceler8 is NTU's employability and personal development programme. It is a flexible award, exclusive to NTU, giving access to a wide range of extracurricular experiences, skills and activities. The award can be completed at the person’s own pace. It's open to all NTU students and recognises and rewards extracurricular experiences and the skills the person has gained. I attended few of Acceler8 organized events. This programme helps you improve your skills and chances to get employed in the future. I learned more about myself in an effective team working session, how I work with others and what is my part in a group. Other events such using LinkedIn and Application masterclass help me to develop a better understanding of what employers looking for and how to improve in the way to be considered for future positions. The hive networking event was useful to build new connections. I really appreciate opportunities Acceler8 offers to learn new skills.
