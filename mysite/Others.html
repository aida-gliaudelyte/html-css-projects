What is it? What i learned and my opinion

  • Digital Horizons offer a range of activities to help explore the tech industry, and what students might do with their degree.
  • Cyber security awarness week in NTU offers variety of different seminars to understand threats on the internet and secure your files.

  • During digital horizons, I had a chance to listen to cybersecurity related seminar, hear about placement opportunities in different companies and even visit one. I learned a bit more about how companies work in the UK and what they expect from the applicants.
