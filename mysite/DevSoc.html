What is it? What i learned and my opinion
Nottingham Trent university Developers society. This society gathers together like-minded tech people. Gives opportunity to meet new people, share programming tips, work in the special DevSoc room, involves in fun activities like gaming nights, creates workshops to improve your knowledge on programming languages, informs and lets you join in computer-related events around the UK. I attended DevSoc organised workshops to expand my current knowledge and learn something new. The workshops were dedicated to Python and Haskell programming languages. I really enjoyed trying out the new more functional Hascall language. Had a great time working in the DevSoc room as well and meeting new people. It's amazing that they give opportunities to attend events outside of university too.
