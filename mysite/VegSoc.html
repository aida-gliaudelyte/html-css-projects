What is it? What i learned and my opinion
Nottingham Trent University Vegetarian and Vegan society. It is open to all NTU students with compassion for animals! Usually meets for fun food-based socials, such as visiting lovely restaurants and learning how to cook interesting meals, plus fun nights out and bar crawls. Attends events for activism, sustainability and shares compassion for animals message with others. Attending Veg Society events were amazing. Meeting new, like-minded people, networking, makes you feel like you are a member of a big, compassionate family. We are having a great time not just organising bar or restaurant visits, but making food together, sharing recipes and watching first vegan GBBO together! I learnt about plastic-free lifestyle and becoming more conscious and sustainable. This great society challenges me to always express myself by involving in serious debates.
